Tazmah Limited

We are an indigenous company that exist primarily to provide micro credit to revenue earners.
We also provide Thrift/Savings club and Cooperative management services to individuals, clubs/societies, and small/medium scale companies.
We are fully registered with Corporate Affairs Commission with RC 1508914.

Our Vision

To be a reliable and easy one-stop fund manager to clients by demystifying the process of accessing and growing funds.

Our Mission

To partner with individuals and groups of revenue earners, bringing them a flexible financial arrangement that they can instantly start using.

Integrity

Our dealings will always be hinged on trust and an easy feedback mechanism.

Professionalism

The right person with the right tools will always be assigned the job.

Value

Our pulse will always be determined by what the customer value.

Our Team

TAZMAH is powered by a team of partners and staffs with different skillset that is united around a common goal - to create value for all stakeholders.

Hamzat Isede

Managing Partner

He is a teacher and researcher (holds a PhD) that has been actively involved in the informal sector of contributory revolving-loan for more than 8 years. He is the CEO of Tazmah Limited, a company that seeks to help clients achieve the financial goals they would otherwise not reach without such services. He has a track record for demonstrating prudence, combined with experience as a thrift-money handler passionate to help revenue earners make their money work for them.
